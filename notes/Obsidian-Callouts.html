Obsidian (rendered 2023-01-14)

Obsidian will render callouts with this syntax ( Use callouts - Obsidian Help):

> [!INFO]
> Here's a callout block.

[!INFO]
Here's a callout block.

[!NOTE]
Here's a callout block.

[!WARNING]
Here's a callout block.

[!DANGER]
Here's a callout block.

[!SUCCESS]
Here's a callout block.
