Build Log-St. Michael Pedestal

Summary

  • Cost: ~$120 + lumber
  • Time: ~16 hr
  • Completed: 2025-02-15-Sat

Design

  • Designed with SketchUp on my 2010 Macbook Pro...this was the project that finally killed it...

Materials Required

  • Oak lumber (various stock and veneer plywood leftovers)
  • $70 Oak Cove Moulding
  • $8 Wood filler
  • $9 Stain (Sedona Red) (1 coat)
  • $20 Wipe-on poly (2 coats)
  • $7 Anti-Skid Gripper pads (couldn't find them, so used some cut-to-size pad I had)

Tools Used

  • big square
  • table saw
  • circular saw
  • miter saw
  • jack plane
  • drill
  • impact driver
  • pocket hole jig (hand-held)
  • nail gun
  • sander

Build Notes

Time Log

  • 2024-10-11-Fri - 1 hr Sketchup design location and rough dimensions
  • 2025-01-11-Sat - 1 hr Sketchup rough design pedestal design + Screenshots to Fr. Paul
  • 2025-01-17-Fri - 30 min Sketchup detail design
  • 2025-01-31-Fri - 2 hr clean, select wood, cut side panels and trim
  • 2025-02-02-Sun - 1.5 hr plane and cut vertical supports, Lowes for trim
  • 2025-02-03-Mon - 1.5 hr assemble base, start base trim
  • 2025-02-04-Tue - .25 hr measure statue at St. Pats
  • 2025-02-08-Sat - 2 hr prep, glue up, and sand top
  • 2025-02-09-Sun - 1.5 hr install top/side trim
  • 2025-02-11-Tue - 0.5 hr install inside trim 2 of 4
  • 2025-02-12-Wed - 1.5 hr install inside trim 4 of 4, drill pocket holes, route and install top
  • 2025-02-13-Thu - 0.5 hr test fit at St. Pat's, install last trim
  • 2025-02-13-Thu - 1 hr fill holes and sand
  • 2025-02-14-Fri - 0.5 hr stain
  • 2025-02-15-Sat - 0.5 hr poly, non-stick feet, add St. Joseph and kid's drawings
  • 2025-02-16-Sun - deliver to St. Patrick Church
