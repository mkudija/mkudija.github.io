## Materials Required
- Oak lumber (various stock and veneer plywood leftovers) 
- $70 [Oak Cove Moulding](https://www.lowes.com/pd/EverTrue-Craftsman-3-4-in-x-8-ft-Unfinished-Cove-Moulding/1000443543)
- $8 [Wood filler](https://www.lowes.com/pd/Minwax-Color-Changing-5-5-oz-Natural-Wood-Filler/1000567879?idProductFound=false&idExtracted=true)
- $9 [Stain (Sedona Red)](https://www.lowes.com/pd/Minwax-Wood-Finish-Sedona-Red-Oil-Based-Interior-Stain-Actual-Net-Contents-8-fl-oz/999913783?idProductFound=false&idExtracted=true) (1 coat)
- $20 [Wipe-on poly](https://www.lowes.com/pd/Minwax-Wipe-On-Poly-Satin-Oil-Based-Polyurethane-Actual-Net-Contents-16-fl-oz/999914303) (2 coats)
- $7 [Anti-Skid Gripper pads](https://www.lowes.com/pd/Scotch-Heavy-Duty-Gripping-Pads-1-5-in-4-pk/1002964354?idProductFound=false&idExtracted=true) (couldn't find them, so used some cut-to-size pad I had)
