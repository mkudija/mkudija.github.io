#### Five Different Takes on Nietzsche
*[[2024-08-10-Sat]] with [[Henry]]*
1. "Gentle Nietzscheans" want to tame him and welcome him into the mainstream (establishment philospohers)
2. "Scremaing Nietzscheans" want to use him to justify their own rant against all order (i.e. Nazis)
3. "Neat Nietzscheans" or deconstructionists who want to play the game of the hermeneutic of suspicion
4. "Nietzsche is nuts" who take him literally as the Antichrist and fight him like a dragon 
5. "Nietzsche as a culture hero" whose example is a warning to our entire culture against the will to power
