The Minimalist Woodworker by Vic Telolin

(Nashville: Spring House, 2015), 151

Workspace essential

  • Power
  • Flooring (soft for feet and dropped tools)
  • Heating/cooling
  • Good lighting

Recommended Tools

Planes

  • Jack plane
  • Shoulder plane
  • Plow plane
  • Router plane

Saws

  • Panel saw
  • Back saw
  • Coping saw

Marking & Measuring

  • Wheel marking gauge
  • Compass
  • Dividers
  • Bevel gauge
  • 12” combination square
  • 24” rule
  • 2” engineer’s square
  • Awl
  • Small snap-off blade knife
  • Pencils

Drilling

  • Brace
  • Auger bits
  • Egg-beater drill
  • Brad-point drill bits

Other

  • Clawhammer
  • Screwdrivers
  • Chisels
  • Chisel mallets
  • Clamps
  • Shop apron

Nice to have

  • Jointer plane
  • Smoothing plane
  • Block plane
  • Rabbet plane
  • Large rip saw
  • Large crosscut saw
  • 4” or 6” adjustable square
  • Chalk line

Power tools to have

  • Bandsaw
  • Thickness planer
  • Drill press
  • Lathe
  • Dust collector
